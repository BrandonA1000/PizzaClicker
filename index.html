Updates Version 1.1.0


    Added 8 new achievements: "Clicker", "Upgrade Collector", "Boss of Managers", "Automation Master", "Michallin Chef", "Corporation", "Monopolizer", and "All achiever".

    - "Clicker": Unlock by reaching 1 PPS.
    - "Upgrade Collector": Unlock by buying 10 of each click upgrade.
    - "Boss of Managers": Unlock by hiring 10 Managers.
    - "Automation Master": Unlock by reaching 200 PPS.
    - "Michallin Chef": Unlock by making 500,000 pizzas.
    - "Corporation": Unlock by making 1,000,000 pizzas.
    - "Monopolizer": Unlock by reaching 1,000 PPS.
    - "All achiever": Unlock by achieving all other achievements.

    Added a save and reset feature.
    Balance Changes:
    - Restaurant PPS increased from 5/sec to 6.5/sec.
    - Manager PPS increased from 15/sec to 17.5/sec.

    New click upgrades added:
    - Upgrade 4: Cost 1000 Pizzas, +13 per click.
    New UI elements added for click upgrades to show how many are owned.
